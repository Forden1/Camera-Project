‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

üîπ How do I add a new camera to my system?
Navigate to the "Add Camera" page from the sidebar, fill in the camera details including IP address and location, select the AI features you want to enable, and click "Add Camera".
üîπ What types of detection does Azka Camera support?
Azka Camera supports three main detection types: Fall Detection for safety monitoring, Quality Control for production lines, and Brawl Detection for security purposes.
üîπ How can I adjust detection sensitivity?
Go to the "Customizations" page where you can adjust sensitivity sliders for each detection type. Higher sensitivity means more alerts but potentially more false positives.
üîπ Can I receive notifications on my phone?
Yes! You can enable SMS, email, and push notifications in the Settings page under the Notifications section.
üîπ What should I do if a camera goes offline?
First, check the physical camera connection and power. Then verify the IP address and network settings. If the issue persists, contact our support team for assistance.
